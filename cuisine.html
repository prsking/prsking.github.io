Explore the Delicious Nepali Cuisine

Nepal's cuisine is a reflection of its diverse ethnic groups, geography, and rich traditions. From the hills to the Terai, Nepali food is known for its vibrant flavors, spices, and hearty ingredients.

The staple food of Nepal is **dal bhat** (lentil soup with rice), which is eaten daily in various forms. However, Nepali cuisine is much more than just dal bhat – it includes a wide variety of dishes, such as **momo** (dumplings), **sel roti** (sweet rice doughnuts), and **gundruk** (fermented leafy greens).

Nepali food also includes many rich curries, chutneys, and street food items that have been influenced by the neighboring cultures of India, Tibet, and China. Whether it's **chatamari** (Nepali pizza) or **thukpa** (noodle soup), Nepali cuisine is diverse and full of flavor!
