The People of Nepal

Nepal is a country of diverse people with a rich cultural heritage. The population consists of various ethnic groups, each contributing to the cultural tapestry of Nepal. From the Sherpas of the Himalayas to the Tharu people of the Terai, Nepal's diverse communities are united by their love for their country and their traditions.

The people of Nepal are known for their hospitality and warmth. No matter where you travel in Nepal, you're likely to experience a genuine warmth from the people you meet. Their culture is deeply influenced by religion, festivals, and a strong sense of community.

The various ethnic groups in Nepal celebrate their traditions with unique customs, festivals, and art forms, making Nepal a melting pot of culture, history, and diversity. The different communities live in harmony, making Nepal one of the most peaceful and inclusive countries in the region.
